INTRODUCTION

MokaHero: Genesis Wars is a next-generation Web3 tactical card game that redefines the concept of "Play-to-Earn". Built on blockchain technology, MokaHero offers a true ownership economy where every card, every piece of land, and every item you earn is truly yours.

Our mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem where entertainment meets finance (GameFi). We prioritize strategic depth and graphical quality, ensuring that MokaHero is fun to play first, and profitable second.

"We are building a digital nation, not just a game. A place where skill dictates value."

STORY & LORE

In the shattered world of Runeterra, the core energy source has fragmented. Four great factions have risen from the ashes to claim dominion over the new world:

  • IGNIS (The Fire Legion): Warriors forged in magma, believing that destruction is the only path to rebirth.
  • SYLVA (The Nature Guardians): Ancient spirits who protect the last remaining forests and life.
  • OMEGA (The Cyber Union): A technologically advanced society that merged flesh with steel.
  • ZAROK (The Void Walkers): Mysterious entities from the abyss, seeking to consume all light.

NFT ASSETS

Assets in MokaHero are minted as NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) on the Polygon/BSC network.

Genesis Heroes

The core units of the game. Each Hero has unique stats (ATK, HP, SPD) and Skills. They come in 4 rarities:

  • Common: Basic starter units.
  • Rare: Enhanced stats, 1 special skill.
  • Epic: High stats, 2 special skills.
  • Legendary: Unique abilities, extremely limited supply.

Lands

Digital real estate in the Moka Metaverse. Landowners can build resource mines to harvest materials required for crafting.

SEMI-ONCHAIN ECONOMY

To ensure sustainability and low gas fees, MokaHero uses a dual-token model connecting Off-chain and On-chain activities.

The Two Currencies

Currency Type Utility
Moka Shards Off-chain (In-game) Earned from PvP/PvE. Used to upgrade Heroes level.
$MOKA Token On-chain (Crypto) The governance token. Tradable on DEX/CEX. Used for Minting & Marketplace.

The "Gate" Mechanism

Players cannot simply "print" money. The economy requires a conversion process:

  1. Play: Grind Dungeons to find NFT Fragments (Non-tradable).
  2. Mint: Combine Fragments + Pay a fee in $MOKA => Mint a full NFT Hero.
  3. Trade: Sell the NFT Hero on the Marketplace for profit.
